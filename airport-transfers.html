FAQ

What you actually need to know.

Most airport guests ask the same handful of questions before booking. Here are the honest answers — no fine print, no surprise fees.

Still unclear? WhatsApp us at +52 987 114 6853 — a real person answers, 24/7.
01

How do I find my driver at the airport?

After you clear customs and exit the terminal, look for the driver holding a sign with your name. We send a WhatsApp message ~1 hour before your flight lands with the driver's name, photo, and vehicle details so you know exactly who to look for.

02

What if my flight is delayed?

We track every arrival in real-time and adjust your pickup automatically. No rescheduling fees, no missed-pickup penalties. Your driver is there whenever you actually walk out — even if you land 4 hours late.

03

Can you take me to my Airbnb or a private address?

Yes — any address on the island. Just paste the property name or street address in the booking notes. If you only have the Airbnb listing name, that works too — our drivers know the island block by block.

04

Do you do round-trip for my departure?

Yes. Round-trip pricing covers both legs (airport → hotel on arrival, hotel → airport on departure). Same driver if available. If your trip is one-way only, the toggle above swaps to one-way pricing.

05

Do you provide child seats or wheelchair-accessible vehicles?

Unfortunately, no — we don't provide child seats or wheelchair-accessible vehicles at this time. If you need either, bring your own portable car seat (we'll secure it) or message us before booking so we can advise you on alternatives.

06

Can we make multiple stops?

Yes. Common request: drop the family at the hotel, then take a couple of guests to dinner or shopping downtown. Add stops in the booking notes — no surcharge for nearby stops, longer detours quoted individually.

07

Do I tip the driver?

Tips are appreciated but not required — your fare covers the full service. Customary tip if you'd like to tip: 10–15% of the fare, in USD or pesos, paid directly to the driver.
