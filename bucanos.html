About Buccanos at Duna

Buccanos at Duna sits on the north end of Cozumel, away from the cruise-day crowds of the southern beach strip. Roughly 4 miles north of the cruise port (10-minute drive). We provide round-trip transfer only — you purchase food and drinks directly at the venue.

Questions

Common questions about Buccanos

Why is Buccanos a good alternative to the southern beach clubs?

It's closer to the cruise port (north side, 4 miles vs 7–9 miles south) and quieter on busy multi-ship days.

Is Buccanos kid-friendly?

Yes — calmer water on the north side and less of a party atmosphere than southern clubs. Good for families with younger kids.

Will my ship's all-aboard still work?

Yes — the shorter drive gives us extra buffer time. Our drivers monitor your ship's all-aboard time and pick you up with at least 60 minutes safety margin.
