Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 May 2026

Cozumel Island Transfers, S. de R.L. de C.V. (“we”, “us”) operates cozu.netlify.app (the “Site”). This page explains what we collect, why, and your rights.

1. What we collect

When you book a transfer through our Site, we collect the information you give us in the booking form:

  • Your name and contact details (email, WhatsApp/phone)
  • Trip details: pickup date and time, return time, cruise ship or flight number, destination, passenger count, special notes

We also collect basic technical data when you visit (IP-derived approximate location, browser type, pages viewed) through Netlify’s server logs and, if enabled, Netlify Analytics. We do not run third-party advertising cookies or trackers.

2. Why we collect it

  • To dispatch your driver and confirm your booking
  • To monitor your cruise ship or flight arrival in real time
  • To contact you about your trip (changes, confirmations, follow-up)
  • To comply with Mexican tax and tourism-operator record-keeping requirements
  • To improve the Site (aggregate analytics, no individual profiling)

3. Who we share it with

We share the minimum necessary with:

  • Our drivers — name, pickup time, destination, ship/flight number, contact phone
  • Netlify (our hosting and form provider, headquartered in the US) — handles Site delivery and booking-form storage
  • Stripe (when payment is enabled in a future release) — processes card payments under their own privacy terms
  • Mexican tax authorities (SAT) when legally required

We do not sell or rent your data. We do not share with cruise lines, beach clubs, or third-party tour operators.

4. How long we keep it

Booking records are retained for 5 years in accordance with Mexican tax and accounting law. Marketing emails (when you opt in) are retained until you unsubscribe. Site analytics are retained for 30 days (Netlify default).

5. Your rights

You can request:

  • A copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction of incorrect data
  • Deletion of your data (where not required for tax/legal retention)
  • Withdrawal of consent to receive marketing

Email hello@cozumelislandtransfers.com with the subject line “Privacy request”. We respond within 15 business days.

6. Children

Our service is sold to adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18 booking on their own behalf.

7. Cookies

We use only essential cookies needed to operate the Site (e.g., remembering your language toggle). If Netlify Analytics is enabled, it uses server-side measurement and does not set tracking cookies. We do not run advertising or social-media re-targeting cookies.

8. International transfers

Our data processors (Netlify, Stripe) are headquartered outside Mexico. By using the Site, you consent to your data being processed in those jurisdictions under their standard contractual protections.

9. Changes to this policy

Material changes will be posted here with a new “last updated” date. Continued use of the Site after a change means you accept the revised policy.
