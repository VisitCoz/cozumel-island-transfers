About KUZÁ Park

KUZÁ is Cozumel's first and only theme park — a hybrid of Caribbean beach and adventure park with water slides, zipline circuits, jungle activities, snorkeling, kayaks, and paddleboards. Day pass packages start at $45 USD (regularly $49) and scale up to $135 with full activity inclusions, lunch buffet, and unlimited domestic drinks. Open Monday–Friday 9 AM to 5 PM, Saturday–Sunday 9 AM to 4 PM (Sundays subject to availability).

Questions

Common questions about KUZÁ

What's the cheapest day pass?

$45 USD (regularly $49) gets you theme park access, beach umbrellas, lounge chairs, nature walks, and cultural experiences. Higher tiers add water park, snorkeling, kayaks, paddleboarding, lunch buffet, and unlimited drinks.

What's El Cielo?

A famous shallow sandbar and starfish snorkeling spot in Cozumel's reef. KUZÁ's higher-tier packages include a guided El Cielo tour.

Are activities included or paid extras?

Depends on the tier. The $45 base is mostly beach + light access; the $85–$135 tiers include the activity menu. Pick the package that matches what you want to do.
