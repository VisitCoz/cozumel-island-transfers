Last updated: 26 May 2026

These terms govern the booking and use of private ground-transfer services provided by Cozumel Island Transfers, S. de R.L. de C.V. (“we”, “us”). By submitting a booking or paying for a transfer, you agree to these terms.

1. The service

We provide pre-booked private vehicle transfers in Cozumel, México, between cruise piers (Puerta Maya, International Pier, Punta Langosta), Cozumel International Airport (CZM), hotels, beach clubs, archaeological sites, and ferry terminals. Vehicles range from 1–40 passenger capacity, all private (not shared).

2. Booking and confirmation

A booking is confirmed when we send a written confirmation by email or WhatsApp. Verbal or in-person agreements alone do not constitute a confirmed booking. Please double-check the date, time, destination, and passenger count on your confirmation.

3. Pricing and payment

  • All prices on the Site are in US Dollars (USD), per vehicle (not per person), and include taxes and tolls.
  • The price quoted at booking is final. No surge pricing, no curb fees.
  • Payment is by credit or debit card at booking (Stripe). We do not collect cash from the driver.

4. Cancellation and refunds

  • Free cancellation if you notify us at least 24 hours before pickup. Full refund processed within 5 business days.
  • Cancellations within 24 hours of pickup are non-refundable. Exceptions: see below.
  • Cruise reroute or port skip: if your ship does not call at Cozumel (for any reason), you receive a full refund — no questions, no fees. We monitor cruise itineraries daily.
  • Flight cancellation or delay > 4 hours: we will reschedule at no extra charge or refund 100% if reschedule isn’t possible.
  • No-show: non-refundable unless you notify us before pickup time.

5. Real-time tracking and on-time guarantee

We track every cruise ship calling at Cozumel via AIS data and every flight via public flight APIs. If your ship is delayed, your driver waits. If your flight is delayed, we reschedule your pickup automatically.

Return-on-time refund: if you book a round-trip cruise transfer and we fail to return you to the pier before your stated all-aboard time (and the delay is our fault, not yours), we refund 100% of the transfer fare. Excludes delays caused by traffic outside our control, road closures, or guest tardiness.

6. Your responsibilities

  • Be at the agreed pickup point at the agreed time. Drivers wait up to 30 minutes; after that, no-show rules apply.
  • Book accurately. Vehicles are sized to your passenger count plus reasonable luggage. Extra passengers may not be accommodated and may forfeit the booking.
  • Behave respectfully toward drivers. We reserve the right to refuse service for intoxicated or abusive passengers, with no refund.
  • Children must be supervised. We do not provide booster or car seats — please bring your own if required (travels free in the vehicle).

7. Vehicle substitution

We reserve the right to dispatch a larger vehicle than booked at no extra charge to you. We do not downgrade without prior agreement.
